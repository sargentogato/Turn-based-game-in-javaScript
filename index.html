Game Rules

  1. Each player can move 3 spaces horizontally or vertically.
  2. Each player start with 100 points of life.
  3. Each player star with a default weapon of 10points power.
  4. All the other weapons have different power.
  5. If a player passes over a box containing a weapon, they leave their current weapon on site and replace it with the new one.
  6. You can change turn pressing enter every time
  7. If one of the player is next to the other, start fight
  8. When in battle you can attack or defend
  9. The damage depends on the player's weapon
  10. The player can choose to attack or defend against the next shot
  11. If the player chooses to defend themselves, they sustain 50% less damage than normal
  12. As soon as the life points of a player (initially 100) falls to 0, they lose. A message appears and the game is over.
